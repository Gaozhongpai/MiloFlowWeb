Always close

Milo is the home base.

Use the Milo bar for quick voice turns, or open the chat history when you want context. Milo can keep a local knowledge base for persistent memory, while Incognito Milo is available when the conversation should disappear.

M

Regular Milo

Persistent chat, compact context, local-first history, optional vault recall, and voice or typed turns.

I

Incognito Milo

No SQLite save, no vault write, no compaction, no archive, and no reusable model branch.
